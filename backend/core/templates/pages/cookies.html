Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how Prestige Cars 24 ("we", "us", "our") uses cookies and similar technologies on prestigecars24.com in compliance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) 2016/679, the ePrivacy Directive 2009/136/EC, and Italian Cookie Law (Provvedimento del Garante n. 229/2014).

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and understand how you interact with them.

Cookies can be:

  • First-party cookies: Set by the website you are visiting (prestigecars24.com)
  • Third-party cookies: Set by other domains (e.g., Google, Yandex, Meta)
  • Session cookies: Deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent cookies: Remain on your device for a set period
2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Ensuring our website functions properly
  • Remembering your preferences and settings
  • Analyzing website traffic and user behavior
  • Measuring the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns
  • Providing personalized content and advertisements
  • Enabling social media features
